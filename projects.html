What Is Reddit?

Although Reddit may not be the go-to for Learning and Teaching in a classroom bases, it could work. Social Media now a days is a major part in our society. It connects on another to discuss share and collaborate our ideas and criticisms.

The way it could be used for Teaching and Learning in a classroom environment or even to expand your general knowledge on a subject is growing each year, such as it can make it easier to share class materials, can influence discussion on certain topics, and make working together easier than it ever has been before.
